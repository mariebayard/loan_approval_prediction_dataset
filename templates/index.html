Welcome to Loan Prediction

On this page, you can use different models built with TensorFlow, Random Forest, XGBoost, and Decision Tree to predict whether a loan is likely to be approved or not.

The models are trained on a dataset from Kaggle. The dataset contains variables such as the number of dependents, education, self-employed status, annual income, loan amount, loan term, credit score, residential assets value, commercial assets value, luxury assets value, and bank asset value. The variable loan status indicates whether the specific loan was approved or not.

You can use the models yourself to predict whether a loan would be approved or not based on the information you provide. The answer you receive is entirely based on the data from the Kaggle dataset.
